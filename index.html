Sprocket Ranch, in Rice, TX

Come hang with us at this beautiful 40 acre property just north of Corsicana.

Sprocket Ranch features an endurocross track for off-road motorcycles and ATVs, two small ponds, a swimming pool, and a large hay field. In addition to hosts Carlin & Sarah Vose, the property is also home to three cows (Skittles, Brownie, and Moo), two dogs (Riley and Frankie--named after Frankie Manning!), and a bunch of chickens.
