Activities

The weekend will be filled with food, social dancing, and outdoor activities. We have some exciting group activities lined up for you.

#1 Weekend long activity: Photo challenge

On Friday check-in, you can pick up a instruction page/list. During the weekend you and your team (of 3-6 people) will attempt to take the photos on a list of weekend challenges. By Sunday morning we'll vote on the favorite photo for each category and post them on social media. The organizers will award prizes on Sunday morning

#2 Night time activity: Bonfire music jam

Gather around a bonfire singing and playing some musical instruments. This is a late night bonding time when you are taking a break from dancing. You can sign up to to bring your favorite acoustic instrument like guitar, ukuleles, harmonica, etc.

#3 Saturday activity: Super Slip’n’Slide

We will lay 75ft of plastic tarping down a mowed, slight incline. We place a sprinkler at the top, put baby shampoo on the slide, and people slide down the hill on an inner tube.
